About

Rooted in the
Maine coast.
Built with heart.

Sunbreak Studio was founded in 2009 by two MECA graduates who believed good design should make people feel something. We've stayed small on purpose. A studio of eight, based in Portland's West End, doing work we're proud to put our name on.

Our clients tend to stay. The average engagement is nine years. We think that says something about how we work: honest, warm, and accountable to the outcome rather than the deliverable.
